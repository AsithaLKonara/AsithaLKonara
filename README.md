## 👋 About Me

I am a **Senior Full-Stack Software Engineer** specialized in engineering production-grade **SaaS platforms**, enterprise **multi-tenant databases**, and **intelligent AI-driven workflows**. Backed by a dedicated commitment to code craftsmanship, my focus is on designing scalable, highly maintainable systems that deliver real-world business impact.

### 🌟 My Core Engineering Principles
* 🧩 **Clean & Modular Architecture** — Adherence to SOLID, OOP, and DRY design principles.
* 📦 **Product-Oriented Engineering** — Shipping complete, resilient, and production-ready applications, not just micro-features.
* 🛡️ **DevOps & Security-First Mindset** — Automated deployments, containerized architectures, secure RBAC, and active telemetry.
* ⚡ **Performance Optimization** — Deep caching strategies (Redis), query tuning, and minimizing execution overhead.
